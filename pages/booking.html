Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about appointments and consultations

How long does a typical appointment last?

Initial consultations typically last 30-45 minutes, while follow-up appointments are usually 15-30 minutes depending on the complexity of your condition.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your ID, insurance card, a list of current medications, any previous dermatology records, and a list of questions or concerns you'd like to discuss.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer telemedicine appointments for certain conditions. Please call our office to determine if your concern is suitable for a virtual consultation.

How far in advance should I book my appointment?

For routine appointments, we recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance. For urgent concerns, we offer same-week appointments when possible.

What is your cancellation policy?

We request that you notify us at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee.
