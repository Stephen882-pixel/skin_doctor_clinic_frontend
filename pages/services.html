Medical Dermatology

Our medical dermatology services focus on the diagnosis and treatment of various skin conditions, from common concerns to complex dermatological diseases. Our board-certified dermatologists use evidence-based approaches to provide effective treatment plans.

Conditions We Treat

  • Acne and acne scarring
  • Eczema and atopic dermatitis
  • Psoriasis and psoriatic arthritis
  • Rosacea and facial redness
  • Skin infections (bacterial, viral, fungal)
  • Hair loss and alopecia
  • Nail disorders
  • Autoimmune skin conditions
  • Allergic skin reactions
  • Chronic skin conditions

Skin Cancer Screening

Early detection saves lives. Our comprehensive skin cancer screening services use advanced technology and expert clinical examination to detect skin cancer in its earliest, most treatable stages.

What We Screen For

  • Melanoma
  • Basal cell carcinoma
  • Squamous cell carcinoma
  • Precancerous lesions
  • Atypical moles
  • Suspicious skin changes

Who Should Get Screened?

  • Adults over 40 years old
  • People with fair skin
  • Those with family history of skin cancer
  • Individuals with many moles
  • People with history of sun exposure
  • Anyone with changing moles

Screening Process

1

Comprehensive examination of all skin areas

2
Dermoscopy

Advanced imaging for detailed mole analysis

3
Documentation

Digital mapping of moles for future comparison

4
Results

Immediate results and follow-up recommendations

Pediatric Dermatology

  • Warts and molluscum contagiosum

  • Psoriasis in children
