Our Story

Dedicated to excellence in dermatological care since 2005

DermaCare Clinic was founded in 2005 by Dr. Sarah Johnson with a vision to provide exceptional dermatological care to patients of all ages. What began as a small practice has grown into one of the leading dermatology clinics in Kenya, known for its patient-centered approach and commitment to excellence.

Our team of board-certified dermatologists combines extensive experience with the latest medical advancements to deliver personalized treatment plans for all skin types and conditions. We believe in a holistic approach to skin health, addressing both medical concerns and aesthetic goals to help our patients look and feel their best.

At DermaCare, we understand that skin conditions can significantly impact a person's quality of life. That's why we are committed to providing compassionate care in a comfortable and welcoming environment. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest technology to ensure accurate diagnoses and effective treatments.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional dermatological care that improves the health and appearance of our patients' skin, enhancing their quality of life and self-confidence.

Our Vision

To be the premier dermatology clinic in East Africa, recognized for clinical excellence, innovative treatments, and outstanding patient satisfaction.

Our Values

  • Excellence in patient care
  • Integrity and professionalism
  • Compassion and empathy
  • Innovation and continuous learning
  • Accessibility and inclusivity

Our Journey

Key milestones in our growth and development

2005

Foundation

DermaCare Clinic was established by Dr. Sarah Johnson in a small office in Nairobi with just two examination rooms.

2008

Expansion

Due to growing demand, we moved to a larger facility and expanded our team to include two additional dermatologists.

2012

Advanced Technology

Invested in state-of-the-art diagnostic and treatment equipment, becoming one of the first clinics in Kenya to offer advanced laser treatments.

2015

Research Initiative

Launched our clinical research department to participate in international studies and contribute to advancements in dermatological care.

2018

Community Outreach

Established our community outreach program, providing free skin cancer screenings and dermatological care to underserved communities.

2023

Present Day

Today, DermaCare is a comprehensive dermatology center with multiple specialists, offering a full range of medical, surgical, and cosmetic dermatology services.
